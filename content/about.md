**CNCK** stands for **Cloud Native Community Korea**, a community of engineers
sharing what they learn while running cloud native systems. This blog is where
we write things down: how a system was built, why a tradeoff was made, and what
broke at 3am.

Topics tend toward Kubernetes, AWS, observability, and the tooling that keeps
production boring.

The site is built with [Zola](https://www.getzola.org) and the **CNCK** theme,
a minimal monochrome design that gets out of the way of the text.
